Tips

  • Do not focus on words. Focus on clear intent.
  • Simplify sentences as a direct message.
  • Do not use contractions.
  • If word missing, try a synonym.
  • Get creative. Build new words from compound words. Example: Gay + Dinosaur = Ostrich
  • Use "may_month" for the month of May.
  • Lexicon is regularly updated so check Lexicon count
